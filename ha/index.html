Tarihi da Asali

1949

A ranar 27 ga Satumba 1949, kwanaki kaɗan kafin kafuwar Jamhuriyar Jama'ar China, Taron Shawara na Siyasa na Jama'ar China ya amince da Jar Tuta mai Taurari Biyar a matsayin tutar ƙasa a hukumance. An fara ɗaga ta a dandalin Tiananmen a ranar 1 ga Oktoba 1949, a bikin shelanta sabuwar jamhuriya.

Zane

Zanen da ya yi nasara na Zeng Liansong ne, masanin tattalin arziki kuma mai zane-zane daga Zhejiang, wanda aka gabatar a gasar ƙasa da ta samu kusan ayyuka 3,000. Zanensa na farko yana da guduma da lauje a cikin babban tauraro; kwamitin zaɓe ya cire su domin zanen ya kasance mai sauƙi kuma ya bambanta da alamomin sauran ƙasashen gurguzu.

2004

An rubuta ƙa'idojin fasaha na hukuma game da gwargwadon tuta, lissafin taurari da launuka a cikin ƙa'idojin ƙasa (GB 12982 don gini, GB 15092 don launi).

Tutocin Tarihi na China

Kafin Jar Tuta mai Taurari Biyar, tutoci mabambanta ne suka kaɗa a China. Waɗannan zane-zane na salo suna nuna fitattun tutoci uku na baya.

Bayanan Fasaha

Gwargwado

3:2

Gwargwadon tsawon tuta zuwa faɗinta.

Grid na Gini

Grid mai raka'a 30 × 20. Tsakiyar babban tauraro yana (5,5), radius na waje 3. Ƙananan taurari suna da radius na waje 1; tsakiyoyinsu suna (10,2), (12,4), (12,7), (10,9).

Launuka

Ja — Pantone 186 C · RGB 222,41,16 · #DE2910
Rawaya — Pantone 012 C · RGB 255,222,0 · #FFDE00

Ƙa'idar hukuma tana bayyana launuka da samfuran zahiri ba lambobin dijital ba — ƙimar hex a allo ƙiyasi ne kawai.

Ƙa'idar Fuskantar Taurari

An juya kowane ƙaramin tauraro yadda ɗaya daga cikin tsininsa zai yi daidai da tsakiyar babban tauraro.

Zanen Launi

Zayyanar tutar China da za a iya bugawa don shafa launi — ya dace da makaranta ko yammacin ruwan sama. Shawara: a shafa wa babban tauraro da ƙananan taurari huɗu launin rawaya ta zinariya a kan jar fage.

Ɗaga Tuta da Ladabi

Ana ɗaga tutar a ranakun hutu na ƙasa, musamman Ranar Ƙasa (1 ga Oktoba) da Makon Zinariya da ke biye, sannan a duk shekara a gine-ginen gwamnati, makarantu da mashigar kan iyaka. Kada tutar ta taɓa ƙasa, kada a ɗaga ta a juye, kuma kada a nuna ta cikin yagaggu ko koɗaɗɗen yanayi. A ranakun makoki na ƙasa ana ɗaga ta rabin sanda.

Tambayoyin da Ake Yawan Yi

Menene ma'anar taurari biyar a tutar China?

Babban tauraron zinariya yana wakiltar jagorancin Jam'iyyar Kwaminis ta China, ƙananan taurari huɗu kuma babbar haɗin kan jama'ar China. Kowane ƙaramin tauraro yana da tsini ɗaya da ke nuni kai tsaye zuwa tsakiyar babban tauraro.

Waɗanne launuka ne tutar China take da su?

Ja da rawaya ta zinariya. Jar fage tana daidai da Pantone 186 C (kimanin #DE2910 a allo), taurari kuma Pantone 012 C (kimanin #FFDE00). Ja tana wakiltar juyin juya hali, rawaya kuma hasken da ke haskaka ƙasa.

Yaushe aka amince da tutar China?

A ranar 27 ga Satumba 1949. An fara ɗaga ta a dandalin Tiananmen na Beijing a ranar 1 ga Oktoba 1949 — ranar da aka shelanta Jamhuriyar Jama'ar China.

Wa ya zana tutar China?

Zeng Liansong, masanin tattalin arziki kuma mai zane-zane daga lardin Zhejiang. Zanensa ya yi nasara a gasar ƙasa da ta samu kusan ayyuka 3,000.

Menene gwargwadon tutar China?

3:2 — tsawon tutar ya ninka tsayinta sau ɗaya da rabi.
